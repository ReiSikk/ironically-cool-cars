ABOUT US

Ironically Cool Car Inc. is a 75 year old family owned business based in Copenhagen currently divided between four brothers.
The company was stared after the war by our great-grandfather under the name "Ironically Dandy Automobiles" with his last savings.
In the following 7 and a half decades his descendants turned a small business that was selling only 2 cars a month into the biggest
ironically cool car selling company in the whole Hovestaden region.
